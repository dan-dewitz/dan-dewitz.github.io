Regional Driver Details

The trucks are based out of Amboy, MN and the truck must return to the yard every weekend. The truck can be based out of somewhere else within 60 miles of Amboy during the week if needed.

Our positions typically include a mix of local and regional work, depending on the season. For example, belly dump work in the summer, hopper work in the winter, and dry vans in the spring.

The right driver will be willing to pull multiple types of trailers, sometimes two or three in the same week. 95% of freight is company direct accounts developed over 35 years of business.

Schedule Details

  • Some weeks will be back to Amboy daily
  • Other trips will require 1-2 nights a week in the truck
  • Some weeks will be Monday through Friday in the truck
  • Winter is our busiest time of the year
  • Home on weekends, one Saturday our Sunday a month required, more available for increased income
  • Be willing to leave the yard by 5am or earlier daily

Compensation

  • Base hourly pay is $22.50 an hour for construction work, raise dicussed after 6 months
  • Prevailing wage on certain jobs
  • 25% of each load while pulling hopper and dry vans increasing 1% each 6-months, cap at 30%
  • Drivers make between $60,000 and $75,000 a year, more possible if desired
  • Starting pay can increase with experience

Perks

  • W2 pay
  • 10 days paid vacation available after 10 days
  • Paper logs
  • Paid weekly, 2 weeks after work is complete
  • $900 minimum pay weekly

Driver Requirements

  • Valid CDL
  • 2 years tractor experience
  • 21 years or old
  • No moving violations in payst 5 years
  • Ability to run a palled jack inside the trailer
  • Ability to climb inside and clean our hoppers and belly dumps
  • Show up on time
  • Complete all paperwork on time
  • Pass a pre-employment drug screening

Owner/Operator Details

Grover Farm is looking to continue to expand out fleet to meet new customer contracts. Started in 1982, we are a multi-generation family-run trucking company out of Amboy, MN. Our primary industries are feed ingredients and seed products throughout the surrounding 5 states around Minnesota. We operate hopper bottom and dry vans.

Perks

  • We provide authority for those that need it, as well as work with O/O that already have their own authority.
  • Year-round, steady, good paying work.
  • 80% customer direct freight, limited brokers
  • Weekly settlements 3 weeks after work is complete
  • Rate transparency, we show rate confirmations and provide detailed list of rates and loads each week
  • Most loads have a fuel surcharge, fuel surcharge and stop fees go 100% to the truck.
  • E-log provided for trucks that require it
  • We allow older equipment with a company inspection
  • Dispatched by the owners who drive themselves still- if we won't haul it, then you won't haul it.

Schedule

Winter is our busiest time of the year- getting seed products out the door. The summer is our slowest time of the year with most people taking vacations with their families. Some weeks will be home daily and other weeks will be gone Monday-Friday. We do not do resets on the road, home every weekend with occasional weekend work for extra income.

Compensation

  • 90% of a gross with your own trailer
  • 8% trailer rent
  • 2% if running our plates and our authority
  • Average of $225,000 a year to the truck at 80%, some make less but are home more, up to the O/O
