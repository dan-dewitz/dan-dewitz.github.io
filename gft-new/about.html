Our Story

Grover Farms Trucking

We are a multi-generational family-owned company based out of Amboy, Minnesota.

1982
Don Grover

Don began operating our first truck, a sweet corn dump truck, in 1982 and slowly added to our fleet.

2010
Rob Grover

Don's son Rob joined the company in 2010 as a driver, and after completing his deployment with the Army National Guard he took over dispatching from his father.

2021
Kate Grover

In 2021, Rob's wife Kate joined the company full-time, primarily working with billing, collecting, and record-keeping.

We love our drivers

We have many drivers that are second-generation drivers, some of which have had their family work here in the past. We take great pride in our mentorship of company drivers to become owner-operators, and help set themselves up for success.

30 trucks hauling out of the upper-midwest

We currently operate about 30 trucks, with a mix of hopper and dry-vans, and primarily haul seed and feed ingredients in the upper-midwest (Minnesota, Iowa, Wisconsin, South Dakota, and North Dakota).

Rooted in agriculture

We are rooted in agriculture and many of our contracts deal with agriculture. We have a strong history of delivering our loads on time and maintaining positive relationships with our customers and those we work with at pick-up and delivery locations.

Organic and specialty loads

We have experience hauling organic products, as well as other specialty loads such as driver-assist, pallet-jacks, and multi-stop loads. We have specialized high-capacity hoppers to deliver low-density products, and battery-powered pallet jacks to assist with farm deliveries.
