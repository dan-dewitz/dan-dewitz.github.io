Trucking Details

The trucks are based out of Amboy, MN and the truck must return to the yard every weekend. The truck can be based out of somewhere else within 60 miles of Amboy during the week if needed.

Our positions typically include a mix of local and regional work depending on the season. For example, belly dump work in the summer, hopper work in the winter, and dry vans in the spring.

The right driver will be willing to pull multiple types of trailers, sometimes two or three in the same week. 95% of freight is company direct accounts developed over 35 years of business.

Schedule Details

  • Some weeks will be back to Amboy daily
  • Other trips will require 1-2 nights a weak in the truck
  • Some weeks will be Monday through Friday in the truck
  • Winter is our busiest time of the year
  • Home on weekends, one Saturday our Sunday a month required, more available for increased income
  • Be willing to leave the yard by 5am or earlier daily

Compensation

  • Prevailing wage on certain jobs
  • 25% of each load while pulling hopper and dry vans increasing 1% each 6-months, cap at 30%
  • Drivers make between 60,000 and 75,000 a year, more possible if desired
  • Starting pay can increase with experience

Perks

  • Paper logs

  • $900 minimum pay weekly
